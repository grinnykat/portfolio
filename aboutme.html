ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Kaitlyn, and I'm sixteen years old! I currently go to Alameda High School. I've always wanted to learn how to code, but never actually pursued it until now. In my free time - literally any free time I may have - I love to draw; it's something I've done constantly since I was little, and continue to do until now. Other than drawing, I also really like writing! All the novels I've completed are old, though, and I try not to think about them because they're so cringe-worthy.
